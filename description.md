## üîí 1. Security & Privacy (The "Safe-Box" Architecture)
In mental health, privacy is #1. CeriNote uses a **10/10 Security Blueprint**:

*   **AES-256-GCM Encryption**: This is military-grade encryption. Every word of your transcripts and reports is encrypted at rest. Even if someone stole the database, they couldn't read a single letter.
*   **Zero-Retention Policy**: By default, CeriNote is "amnesic." It deletes the raw audio recording the second it finishes converting it to text. No patient voices are stored on our servers.
*   **Mandatory Digital Consent**: You cannot start a recording without the system logging that the patient has given consent. This creates a legal audit trail for your protection.
*   **Hardened Infrastructure**: We use advanced web security (HSTS, CSP, and XSS protection) to ensure that only you can access your clinical workspace.
*   **Audit Logging**: Every single action‚Äîwhenever a report is viewed, deleted, or generated‚Äîis logged with a timestamp for forensic clinical accountability.

## üß† 3. Clinical Intelligence Core (The "AI Brain")

### üìÑ Professional Reporting
